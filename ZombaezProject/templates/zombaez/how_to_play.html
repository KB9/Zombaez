You start the game on a street. Your aim is to go into houses and get supplies and save other survivors. To enter a house, press the 'E' key while on a door.

When you are inside a house you have many rooms to choose from. To enter a room you must press 'E'. Inside you will find zombies or loot!

If you are lucky and find no zombies then you will be told what items you have collected.
